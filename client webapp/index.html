The Vision

Our solution is to ordering and picking up pharmaceutical prescriptions lies in the skies. Targeted at small-business and family pharmacies, we want to expedite and simplify the process of picking up your monthly, weekly, or daily prescription. Using autonomous drone technology developed in-house, we are able to dispatch a drone to your location after you make your prescription order online. Within 30 minutes, our aerial technology will transport and then deliver your order to your location, verifying your identity and your delivery on arrival.

Team Bio

We are a team of college students from both Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University and the University of Florida. Our project was inspired by the idea of making drone delivery available to all consumers and business-owners, not just big business companies and organizations. Our solution uses easily accessible resources, particulary the Parrot A.R. Drone 2.0 Elite Edition and any laptop computer with an internet connection.
